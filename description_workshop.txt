An unofficial addon to the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2815540735&searchtext=Fargo%27s+Souls+Mod] Fargo's Souls Mod[/url] that makes it compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2843112914&searchtext=Secrets+of+the+Shadows] Secrets of the Shadows[url] & [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2864843929&searchtext=consolaria] Consolaria[url].

The mod can be played with any assortment of the supported mods, all of them, or none at all, as the content is dynamically loaded depending on what mods you currently have enabled.

v0.1 includes support for Pre-Hardmode, and the mod will continue to be worked on and gain regular updates.

Currently supports:
[list]
	[*] Secrets of the Shadows
	[*] Consolaria
	[*] Heartbeataria
[/list]

Features:
[list]
	[*] Eterity mode reworks
	[*] Secrets of the Shadows & Consolaria Enchantments, Forces, & Souls
	[*] Fargo's Mutant quality of life support such as banner recipes & alternate summons.
	[*] Merged accessories trees such as the boot crafting tree or shield crafting tree with Fargo's Souls Mod accessories
	[*] Support for other Fargo's addons such as the Calamity - Fargo's Souls DLC.
[/list]

Planned Features:
[list]
	[*] Spooky Mod & Rise of Ages compatibility (maybe some day)
[/list]
